HOSTEL FACILITIES

Consultation and distribution of medicines to students Organization of different camps and talks for benefit and awareness of students Sick Rooms are specially designed and are well equipped with the required medical kits- For emergency primary treatment First aid facility is available in every department Medical checkup camps are regularly organized for students

SECURITY

PBR Visvodaya Institute of Technology & Science has set up a medical facility with necessary medical facilities in the campus to give first aid in case of any medical emergency. First aid boxes are available in every individual department. A general physician visits the campus regularly for the convenience of students with health issues. The institute also arranges a free medical check up annually for students and staff. Health awareness camps and talks are conducted regularly for the benefit of the students.
